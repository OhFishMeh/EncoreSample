About Encore

Encore Restaurant is designed as a stage for connection—where every plate, every glass, and every conversation feels intentional. By day, we welcome relaxed lunch dates and business meetups. By night, the lights dim, the candles glow, and the room transforms into an intimate, elegant setting for unforgettable dinners.

Our chefs blend classic techniques with modern creativity, using fresh, seasonal ingredients to craft dishes that are both familiar and surprising. Whether you’re joining us for a quiet lunch, a romantic evening, or a fully catered celebration, Encore is where the night begins—and where memories ask for an encore.

Lunch & Dinner Experiences

From mid-day conversations to late-night celebrations, Encore offers distinct experiences tailored to the rhythm of your day.

Lunch at Encore

Bright flavors, fresh ingredients, and a relaxed atmosphere make Encore the perfect setting for lunch dates, business meetings, or a quiet solo escape. Enjoy thoughtfully crafted dishes that energize without rushing you out the door.

Dinner at Encore

As the sun sets, Encore shifts into a romantic, candlelit space with soft music, warm golden light, and elegantly plated entrées. Ideal for anniversaries, first dates, and nights that deserve to be remembered.
